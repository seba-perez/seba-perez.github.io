Summary

Sebastián Pérez is an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Sciences of Universidad de Santiago de Chile (USACH). He obtained his PhD in Astrophysics from the University of Oxford and a BSc in Science with a major in Astronomy from Universidad de Chile.

His research focuses on planet formation, including disk kinematics as a probe of embedded planets, planet–disk interactions, and substructures in protoplanetary and debris disks. He is also actively involved in interdisciplinary projects connecting science, art, and education.
